The Struggle at Peksiminas 2024: Achieving 8th Place Nationally

PEKSIMINAS 2024.

The year 2024 became one of the most memorable moments in my journey as an Information Technology student at Sam Ratulangi University. I had the opportunity to participate in Peksiminas 2024, a national-level student art competition held at the State University of Jakarta from September 2-6, 2024.

Starting from the university-level selection to the regional level, I qualified for the nationals, representing North Sulawesi. Competing against talented students across Indonesia taught me resilience and creativity. I secured 8th place nationally!
