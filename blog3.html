FLS2N Solo Guitar 2022: A Melodic Journey

FLS2N 2022.

Back in 2022, I took part in the FLS2N (Festival dan Lomba Seni Siswa Nasional) for the solo guitar category at the provincial level, held in Manado, North Sulawesi, on August 15-17, 2022. Music has always been a significant part of my life, and performing on stage with my guitar was a dream come true.

Preparing for FLS2N meant countless hours of practice, perfecting my piece, and managing stage fright. The competition was intense, with incredibly talented musicians from across the province.
