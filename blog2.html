ONMIPA-PT 2024: A Valuable Experience at ONMIPA 2024


ONMIPA-PT 2024.

In 2024, I took on an academic challenge at Olimpiade Nasional MIPA Perguruan Tinggi (ONMIPA) 2024. I chose Mathematics because it has been one of my strengths since high school. The journey began with an internal selection at Sam Ratulangi University. I achieved 2nd place at the university level and advanced to the provincial level in North Sulawesi.

At the provincial level, I trained hard, studying past questions and problem banks from BPTI. The regional selection was held online on April 24-25, 2024. I was thrilled to reach this stage, though there was a slight disappointment as I didn’t qualify for the nationals, which took place at Hasanuddin University in May 2024. Only the 1st-place winners from each region and a few top ranks advanced, and I fell just short of the quota.

Even so, this experience taught me about strategy and precision. I plan to try again next year! See My Provincial Certificate
